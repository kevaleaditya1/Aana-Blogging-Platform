The iPhone 16 lineup is still a few months away, but leaks are already pouring in. The most exciting rumor is the introduction of an "Ultra" model.

## Buttonless Design?

Reports suggest that Apple might finally ditch physical buttons in favor of solid-state haptic buttons.

## Periscope Zoom

The Ultra model is expected to feature a 10x periscope zoom lens, catching up to Samsung's Ultra series.

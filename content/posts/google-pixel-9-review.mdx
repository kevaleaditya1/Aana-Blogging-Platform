The Google Pixel 9 Pro is finally here, and it's making bold claims about AI integration. With the new Tensor G5 chip, Google is pushing the boundaries of what a smartphone can do.

## Design and Display

The design has been refined with flatter edges and a more premium glass back. The camera bar is still there, but it looks more integrated than ever.

## AI Features

Gemini Nano is at the core of the experience. From summarizing emails to generating images on the fly, the AI features are genuinely useful.

## Camera

As expected, the camera is top-tier. The new 50MP main sensor captures incredible detail, even in low light.

## Verdict

If you want the smartest phone on the market, the Pixel 9 Pro is it.
